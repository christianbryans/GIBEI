Galeri Investasi Bursa Efek Indonesia Telkom University

Serves as a platform to introduce the Capital Market and explore career aspirations in the Financial Industry at an early stage to the Telkom University academic community. It aims to facilitate learning in various fields, such as investment, stock trading, and its practical application, aspiring to bridge the gap between mastering knowledge and its practical application in the capital market and financial industry professionals.

Vision

To build a high-value organization, to provide education and cultivating community who can create significant impact within the industry.

Mission

  • Provide an experience to upgrade a hard and soft skill quality in the industry.
  • Provide experience to upgrade a skill set to enter the industry.
  • Provide experience to upgrade a hard skill set to enter the industry.
