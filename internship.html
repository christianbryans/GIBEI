Program Timeline

Our structured 6-month internship program is designed to progressively build your skills and knowledge in finance and investment.

Month 1-2
Foundation & Orientation
Introduction to capital markets, basic financial concepts, and GIBEI operations. Complete orientation and initial training modules.
Month 3-4
Practical Application
Hands-on experience with market analysis, research projects, and client interaction under supervision of senior staff.
Month 5
Specialization
Focus on specific areas of interest such as equity research, portfolio management, or investment advisory services.
Month 6
Final Project & Evaluation
Complete a capstone project, present findings to senior management, and receive final evaluation and certification.
